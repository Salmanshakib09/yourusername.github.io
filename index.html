Research Project

Thermal Care of Newborns in Bangladesh

This research project investigated socio-economic and demographic factors associated with thermal care practices for newborns in Bangladesh using the Bangladesh Demographic and Health Survey 2017–18.

The project focused on public health data analysis and examined how demographic and socio-economic characteristics are related to newborn thermal care practices.

  • Dataset: Bangladesh Demographic and Health Survey 2017–18
  • Methods: Descriptive statistics, chi-square tests, and regression analysis
  • Research area: Applied statistics, biostatistics, maternal and child health
  • Tools: R, Stata, SPSS, and Microsoft Excel
